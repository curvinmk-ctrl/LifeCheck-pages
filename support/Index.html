Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add items to my checklist?

Tap the "Add" tab at the bottom of the screen. You can create custom items with names, schedules, and reminder times. You can also use Quick Add on the home screen for common items.

How do notifications work?

Go to Settings and enable Push Notifications. You can set specific reminder times for each checklist item when you create or edit them.

What's included in Premium?

Premium unlocks Weather-Based Reminders, Quick Notes, Routine Templates, Stats & Progress tracking, Lost & Found, Shared Checklists, and Location-Based Reminders. It also removes all ads.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings, tap "Go Premium", and then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the paywall screen.

Is my data backed up?

LifeCheck stores all data locally on your device. Your data is included in your device's iCloud backup if enabled. If you delete the app, your data will be removed.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > LifeCheck to manage or cancel your subscription.
